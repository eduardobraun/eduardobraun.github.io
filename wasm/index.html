    WebAssembly

    Here you will find posts related to WASM.

    WASM was originally created to be a portable binary-code format for the web. But it turns out that running binary-code in a sandboxed environment is great for many other applications. One example is network proxys, like Envoy, that usually provide a way to do advanced filtering of requests. In the case of Envoy originally these filters were programed in LUA, but recently support for WASM filters was added.
